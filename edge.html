Guindy's gateway to connectivity and convenience

EDGE is located in Guindy, one of the most prestigious and prominent neighborhoods in Chennai, often referred to as the Gateway to Chennai. Guindy is home to several residential projects and commercial properties, as well as many of the city's famous landmarks, including the Guindy National Park. The area boasts excellent infrastructure, with hospitals, retail outlets, SEZs and tech parks, and schools all within close proximity. Additionally, Guindy is well-connected to major roads such as Anna Salai Road, Sardar Patel Road, and Alandur Road. For transportation, Guindy Railway Station, Chennai International Airport, Guindy Metro Station, and Little Mount Metro Station are just minutes away from the locality.
